How it was trained

Pretrained on up to 9 trillion tokens from publicly available data. The small 1B/3B models were created by pruning larger Llama models and then recovering quality through knowledge distillation — the logits of the much larger Llama 3.1 8B and 70B models were used as token-level teaching targets. The chat model was then aligned with several rounds of supervised fine-tuning (SFT), rejection sampling, and Direct Preference Optimization (DPO).

Why this model matters

Llama 3.2 3B was purpose-built for on-device and edge AI — small enough to run in a browser tab or a phone, yet capable enough for real summarization, rewriting, and Q&A. Distillation lets it punch well above its size class. Because the weights are openly available, apps like this one can run a genuine LLM entirely on your machine with no server and full privacy — which is the whole reason "Chat with a Doc" can exist.
